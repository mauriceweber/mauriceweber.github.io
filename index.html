About me

I am a third year PhD student at ETH Zurich’s DS3Lab, supervised by Professor Ce Zhang.

My research interests revolve around the reliability of machine learning systems and quantum algorithms. In the context of ML systems, I am interested in reasoning about robustness guarantees for input perturbations and data distribution shifts. In the area of quantum computation, I work on establishing principled approaches to reason about reliability in noisy scenarios.

I hold a BSc and an MSc in Mathematics, both from ETH Zürich, where I focused on statistics and machine learning and graduated in 2019 with distinction.

Feel free to contact me by email, on Twitter or on LinkedIn.
